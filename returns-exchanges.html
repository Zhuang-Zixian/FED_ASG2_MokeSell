Returns & Exchanges

Need to return or exchange a product? Follow our easy steps below.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can request a return within 14 days of receiving the item.

Eligibility for Returns:
  • Item must be unused, in original packaging, and with all included accessories.
  • Returns must be requested within 14 days of delivery.
  • Some items such as perishable goods, customized products, or digital downloads may not be eligible for returns.

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to process your return:

  1. Go to Your Orders in your MokeSell account.
  2. Select the item you wish to return and click Request Return.
  3. Choose the reason for the return and upload any supporting images (if applicable).
  4. Print the return shipping label (if provided) and pack the item securely.
  5. Ship the item back to the seller within the return window.

Exchange Policy

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size, color, or variant, please follow the return process above and place a new order for the replacement item.

Note: Exchanges are subject to item availability. If the desired replacement is unavailable, a refund will be issued.

Refund Processing

Once your return is received and inspected, refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Refund Methods:
  • Credit/Debit Card - 3-7 business days
  • PayPal - 1-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer - Up to 10 business days

Frequently Asked Questions

If you receive a defective item, please report it within 48 hours of delivery. The seller may offer a replacement or refund.

Return shipping costs depend on the reason for the return. If the item is defective or incorrect, the seller will cover the return cost. Otherwise, the buyer is responsible.
